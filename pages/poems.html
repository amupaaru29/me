Girls are behind the Screens

For a month I’ve been touring
Around the remote villages developing
Just to know what they think
And what the girls there want to think

They told they want them,
The girls, to stitch all their clothes per hem
But the girls said they want to study
Even when the season is rainy

The elders have their own traditions
Giving the girls too many conditions
Oh, the girls were just too helpless
For the money they had too less

In India, the population of boys
Has dominated the girls’ as toys
Though the govt. has passed laws
For a girl to live with no flaws

The girls are still behind the screens
Why don’t we just open those screens
Let the girls come out of their cage
And dance and win out on the stage!
